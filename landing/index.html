Simple Piskel-Clone

Piskel is a free online editor for animated sprites & pixel art.
Create animations in your browser.

Implemented functionality overview:

  • Different tools
  • Work with frames (copy, paste, delete)
  • Animation preview, fullscreen, frame rate change
  • Export as apng / gif
